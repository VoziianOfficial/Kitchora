Service detail

A full kitchen request can bring several project parts into one clearer provider conversation.

Full kitchen remodelling requests often include more than one area of the room. A homeowner may be considering cabinet replacement, countertop updates, backsplash tile, lighting, fixtures, flooring transitions, island function, pantry storage, or layout changes. Kitchora helps organize those details into a comparison-ready request.

This category is most useful when your project feels broader than a single surface or fixture. Participating providers may ask follow-up questions about measurements, site conditions, materials, design decisions, permits, timelines, and installation requirements. Kitchora does not control those provider-specific details.

Final pricing, scheduling, warranties, licensing, insurance, and service terms are provided by participating providers. Homeowners should verify all details directly before hiring.

What to include

Details that can help frame a full kitchen remodelling request.

You do not need perfect measurements to start, but specific notes can help participating providers understand the project direction and ask better follow-up questions.

Approximate kitchen size and layout type
Current issues with storage, lighting, surfaces, or flow
Cabinet, counter, backsplash, lighting, and fixture preferences
Whether the footprint should stay similar or change significantly
Timeline comfort, planning stage, and preferred provider response method
Questions about warranty, licensing, insurance, cleanup, and written scope
