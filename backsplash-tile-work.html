Service detail

Backsplash and tile requests can focus provider conversations around style, surface prep, coverage, and finish details.

Backsplash and tile work requests may involve a small feature wall, a full counter-to-cabinet backsplash, decorative tile, neutral tile, grout color, trim pieces, outlet cutouts, wall preparation, or tile removal. Kitchora helps homeowners organize those details before comparing provider responses.

This category is useful when tile is the main focus, even if countertops, cabinets, lighting, or fixtures may be part of the surrounding kitchen update. Participating providers may ask for photos, wall dimensions, tile preferences, existing surface conditions, and installation expectations before discussing scope or timing.

Kitchora does not sell tile, install backsplash, repair walls, or guarantee provider work. Final material selections, pricing, scheduling, warranties, licensing, insurance, and service terms are provided by participating providers.

What to include

Details that can help frame a backsplash or tile work request.

You do not need a final tile plan to start. Basic details can help participating providers understand the wall area, design direction, and potential prep questions.

Approximate backsplash height, wall length, and range or sink areas
Existing backsplash condition, wall damage, or removal questions
Preferred tile color, shape, pattern, grout tone, and trim detail
Outlet locations, corners, shelves, range hoods, or special cutout areas
Whether countertops, cabinets, lighting, or fixtures may also be affected
Questions about prep work, written scope, warranty, cleanup, licensing, and insurance
