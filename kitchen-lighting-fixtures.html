Service detail

Lighting and fixture requests can focus provider conversations around function, ambience, placement, and compatibility.

Kitchen lighting and fixture requests may involve under-cabinet lighting, pendant lights, ceiling fixtures, task lighting, dimming goals, sink fixtures, cabinet hardware coordination, outlet or switch questions, and how lighting should support cooking, dining, and storage zones.

This category is useful when lighting or fixtures are the main concern, even if cabinetry, counters, tile, or island details may also be part of the conversation. Participating providers may ask about existing wiring, fixture locations, ceiling conditions, appliance zones, and whether licensed trade involvement is required.

Kitchora does not install lighting, perform electrical work, sell fixtures, or guarantee provider work. Final pricing, scheduling, warranties, licensing, insurance, and service terms are provided by participating providers.

What to include

Details that can help frame a kitchen lighting or fixture request.

You do not need final electrical plans to start. Basic notes can help participating providers understand the desired lighting effect, fixture areas, and practical questions.

Current lighting concerns, dark zones, glare, or outdated fixtures
Desired fixture types such as pendants, recessed lights, or under-cabinet lighting
Island, sink, prep area, dining zone, or cabinet-run placement notes
Preferred finish direction such as copper, matte black, brass tone, cream, or warm neutral
Questions about wiring, switches, dimmers, fixture compatibility, or licensed trade requirements
Questions about written scope, warranty, cleanup, licensing, insurance, and scheduling
