Our platform

Designed for homeowners who want a clearer starting point before provider conversations.

A kitchen remodelling request can include layout ideas, cabinetry, surface materials, lighting, backsplash details, island needs, storage goals, and budget comfort. Kitchora helps organize those details into a request that can be shared for provider-matching purposes.

We do not remodel kitchens directly. We do not send our own installers, guarantee quotes, sell materials, or control provider schedules. The homeowner remains in control of whether to continue.
