Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Kitchora uses cookies, localStorage, and similar technologies on this independent kitchen remodelling provider-matching platform.

1. Platform role

Kitchora is an independent kitchen remodelling aggregator and provider-matching platform. We help homeowners organize kitchen project details and compare available local provider options.

Kitchora does not remodel, install, design, inspect, repair, manufacture, sell, or guarantee kitchen work directly. Provider availability, pricing, warranties, licensing, insurance, scheduling, and service terms are provided by participating providers.

2. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites. They can help a website remember preferences, support functionality, understand general usage patterns, and improve the browsing experience.

Similar technologies may include localStorage, sessionStorage, pixels, tags, and scripts that help store preferences or understand how visitors use the website.

3. How Kitchora uses cookies and localStorage

We may use cookies and localStorage for the following purposes:

  • Remembering your cookie consent choice
  • Supporting website navigation and form usability
  • Improving page performance and user experience
  • Understanding general interaction patterns on the website
  • Helping maintain website security and spam prevention
  • Supporting administrative, compliance, and troubleshooting needs

4. Types of cookies we may use

Essential cookies

Essential cookies and storage tools help the website function properly. For example, the cookie banner may use localStorage to remember whether you accepted or declined cookies. These tools are generally necessary for basic site operation.

Preference cookies

Preference cookies may remember choices such as cookie consent status or interface preferences so that the website does not ask for the same choice repeatedly.

Analytics or performance cookies

Analytics or performance cookies may help us understand general browsing patterns, such as which pages are visited and how users interact with the website. Where used, this information helps improve website clarity and usability.

Security and anti-spam tools

We may use technical tools, form fields, server-side checks, or similar measures to help reduce spam, automated submissions, misuse, or technical abuse of the request form.

5. Cookie consent choices

The website includes a cookie banner with Accept and Decline options. Your choice is stored so the banner does not keep appearing on every page.

Declining optional cookies may limit analytics or preference-related features, but basic navigation and access to important legal pages should remain available.

6. Managing cookies in your browser

You can control, block, or delete cookies through your browser settings. Browser controls vary, so review the help section of your browser for instructions.

If you delete cookies or localStorage, the website may ask for your cookie preference again and some saved preferences may be reset.

7. Third-party tools

Some website features, scripts, embedded libraries, hosting tools, analytics tools, or security tools may be provided by third parties. These third parties may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Kitchora is not responsible for third-party cookie practices, third-party websites, provider websites, or external services.

8. Request form and technical data

When you submit a request form, technical information may be processed to help deliver the form, reduce spam, troubleshoot errors, and maintain records. The form also includes a honeypot anti-spam field designed to identify automated submissions.

Form data may include your name, email, phone number, selected kitchen category, project message, source page, and consent confirmation. For more detail, read our Privacy Policy.

9. Updates to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the website after updates means you acknowledge the updated policy.
